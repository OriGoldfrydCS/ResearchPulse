# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Our standards

We strive to create a positive community. Examples of behavior that contributes to a healthy environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate in language and actions
- Assuming good intent and giving constructive feedback
- Welcoming different viewpoints and experiences
- Focusing on what’s best for the project and its users

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks, insults, or deliberate intimidation
- Sexualized language or unwanted sexual attention
- Trolling, doxxing, or publishing others’ private information
- Sustained disruption of discussions or collaboration

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, code review, and other community channels) as well as when representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate and fair corrective action.

Actions may include (but are not limited to):

- Asking for a change in behavior
- Removing or editing content (comments, issues, PRs)
- Temporarily limiting participation
- Permanently banning from participation
